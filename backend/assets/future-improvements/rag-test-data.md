## About North Coast-Sahel
**Source**: [nawy.com/area/north-coast-sahel](https://www.nawy.com/area/north-coast-sahel)

1. **Question**: Which two international airports provide air travel access to the North Coast (Sahel) region?

   **Answer**: The two nearby airports are Borg El Arab International Airport and Marsa Matrouh International Airport.

2. **Question**: What is the approximate driving time from Cairo to the North Coast, and how does the distance change between New Cairo and El Sheikh Zayed?

   **Answer**: It is approximately 2.5 hours away from Cairo. The distance to New Cairo is 254 km, while it is closer to El Sheikh Zayed at 196 km.

## Family Park El Rehab Cairo: Its Tickets, Activities & Opening Hours
**Source**: [nawy.com/blog/128157-family-park-el-rehab-cairo-its-tickets-activities-opening-hours](https://www.nawy.com/blog/128157-family-park-el-rehab-cairo-its-tickets-activities-opening-hours)


1. **Question**: Where exactly is Family Park located, and which major road is it situated off?

   **Answer**: Family Park is located inside Al Rehab City, just off Suez Road in New Cairo.

2. **Question**: What are the specific ticket prices for adults on weekdays versus weekends?

   **Answer**: The ticket price is 150 EGP per person on weekdays (Sunday – Wednesday) and 250 EGP per person on weekends (Thursday – Friday).

3. **Question**: At what age are children granted free entry to the park?

   **Answer**: Children under 4 years old receive free entry.

4. **Question**: Name three specific dining or food options mentioned that are located inside the park.

   **Answer**: The text mentions the Forest Restaurant, Lake Restaurant, and Rendezvous Cafeteria.

5. **Question**: What are the official daily opening and closing hours for Family Park?

   **Answer**: The park is open from 9:00 AM to 10:00 PM.
